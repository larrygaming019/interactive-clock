# ⚙️ Interactive & Customizable Analog Clock

Hey there! Welcome to the Interactive Analog Clock project. 

This is a feature-packed, highly customizable HTML5 clock built completely with **vanilla HTML, CSS, and JavaScript** (no heavy frameworks or dependencies required!). Whether you want to play around with time physics, design a sleek custom timepiece, or keep track of multiple time zones, this app has you covered.

---

## ✨ What Can It Do?

### 🕒 Hands-On Timekeeping
* **Interactive Dragging:** Grab any hand—hours, minutes, seconds, or sub-dials—and spin it around to inspect specific times.
* **Realistic Physics:** Built-in spring and magnetic physics pull the hands back smoothly when you let go.
* **Sub-Dials & Indicators:** Keeps track of milliseconds, the day of the week, and the month of the year.
* **Digital & Counter Displays:** Prefer numbers? Toggle between a clean digital readout (12h/24h) or a rolling mechanical counter.

---

### 🎨 Personalization & Styles
* **Built-in Themes:** Instantly swap styles—choose from Default, Minimal, Retro, Futuristic, Dark, or Light mode.
* **Custom Clock Faces:** Want a personal touch? Upload your own photos, custom graphics, or SVGs right onto the clock dial.
* **Fine-Tuned Styling:** Tweak colors, tick marks, frame borders, and visual glow effects to build your ideal clock.

---

### 🌍 Extra Goodies
* **World Clocks:** Track time across the globe by adding secondary time zone dials side-by-side.
* **Audio FX:** Turn on authentic mechanical ticking sounds (with adjustable volume, so it doesn't drive you crazy!).
* **Time Machine Controls:** Pause time, fast-forward, rewind, or set custom simulation speeds.

---

## ⌨️ Keyboard Shortcuts

Quickly control your clock right from your keyboard:

| Key | Action |
| :--- | :--- |
| <kbd>E</kbd> | Reset the seconds hand directly to **0** |
| <kbd>M</kbd> | Cycle dial markers (*All Markers & Numbers* → *Hour Markers Only* → *Clean / Hidden*) |
| <kbd>Space</kbd> | Toggle Pause / Resume at **1x speed** |
| <kbd>K</kbd> | Toggle Pause / Resume at **your previous simulation speed** |

---

## 🚀 Getting Started

No build steps, package managers, or local servers needed!

1. **Clone or download** this repository.
2. Double-click `index.html` to open it in your favorite web browser.
3. Start tweaking, dragging, and using your keyboard shortcuts!

*Feel free to fork, experiment, and make it your own!*
